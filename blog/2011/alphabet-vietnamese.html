Operation Learn-To-Read-Vietnamese: Alphabet

Aug 03, 2011

Like most other languages, learning the alphabet is one of the first steps to reading so this is where I'll start. Here's a vietnamese alphabet list I found online to study:

VowelsPronunciation Example
a'ah' as in father
â'uh' as in sofa
ă'a' as in hat
e'e' as in get
ê'eh' as in prey
i'i' as in machine (north), 'i' as in bit (south)
o'aw' as in hot or saw
ô'oa' as in boat
ơ'uh' as in fur or mug
u'oo' as in boot
ư'ue' as in hurry
y'ee' as in bee


ConsonantsPronunciation Example
b'b' as in big
c'c' as in cat
ch'ch' as in church
d'z' as in zoo (north), 'y' as in yes (south)
đ'd' as in done
g'g' as in good
gh'g' as in good
gi'z' as in zoo (north), 'y' as in yes (south)
h'h' as in house
k'c' as in cat
kh'ch' as in the German Bach
l'l' as in life
m'm' as in man
n'n' as in nice
ng'ng' as in singer
ngh'ng' as in singer
nh'ny' as in canyon
p'p' as in stop
ph'f' as in find
qu'qu' as in queue
r'z' as in zoo (north), 'r' as in ring (south)
s's' as in sore (north), 's' as in sure (south)
t't' as in stop
th't' as in top
tr'ch' as in church (north), 'tr' as in tree (south)
v'v' as in van (north), 'y' as in yes (south)
x's' as in sore

* Grayed out items are equivalent to english
