Photos

Growing up, I was pretty camera shy. Perhaps because I was introverted and awkward. Or because my elders always chose the most embarassing moments to capture.

Now, when I look back on those photos, I'm grateful those little moments of time were frozen for me. In just a single photo, a flood of memories and emotions can overwhelm me in ways words cannot describe.

Some things are better explained through pictures. So, instead of adding more words to my About section, I hope to share a better view of myself and interests through some photos of people, moments, and accomplishments special to me.
