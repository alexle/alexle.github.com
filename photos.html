Photos

Growing up, I was pretty camera shy. Perhaps because I was introverted and awkward. Or because my family and friends always chose the most embarassing moments to capture.

Now, when I look back on those photos, I'm grateful those little moments of time were frozen for me. In just a single photo, a flood of memories and emotions can overwhelm me in ways words cannot describe.

Some things are better explained through pictures. So, instead of adding more words to my About section, here is an extended view of myself and interests through some photos of people, moments, and accomplishments special to me.
